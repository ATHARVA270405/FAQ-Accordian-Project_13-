What is your return policy?

You can return any item within 30 days of purchase.

  1. Return Window: Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase.
  2. Condition: Returned items must be in their original condition with tags intact.
  3. Proof of Purchase: A receipt or order confirmation is required for processing returns.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping usually takes 5-7 business days.

  1. Standard Shipping: Takes 5-7 business days for delivery.
  2. Expedited Shipping: Available with delivery in 2-3 business days.
  3. Same-Day Shipping: Available in select locations if the order is placed before 12 PM local time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.

  1. Credit/Debit Cards: We accept all major credit and debit cards
  2. Bank Transfers: Available for select countries.
  3. Gift Cards: You can use our store gift cards for purchases.

    4. Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide.

    1. Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries worldwide.
    2. Delivery Time: International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days, depending on the destination.
    3. Shipping Costs: Costs vary by location and will be calculated at checkout.

      4. How do I track my order?

      Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information.

      1. Order Confirmation: Once your order is placed, you will receive a confirmation email with your order details.
      2. Shipping Notification: After your order is shipped, we will send you a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number.
      3. Tracking Link: Use the tracking number to track your package through the carrier's website (e.g., UPS, FedEx, DHL).
